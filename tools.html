These are the main tools. Anything sent through the United States Postal Service is considered true in a court of law. That is why the penalties for mail fraud are so severe.

This means when negotiating with any company it is highly beneficial for you to do so in writing through the United States Postal Service.

This will give you a written record of all of the steps you have made in good faith if they must be presented in the future.

Do not write anything you specifically know to be untrue because that could be considerd fraud and is unnecessary.
